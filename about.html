About the TVL Strand

This page provides an overview of the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) strand, its offered specializations, and the purpose of this student output showcase.

Overview of the TVL Strand

The Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) strand is a Senior High School track that focuses on the development of practical skills and technical competencies. It is designed to prepare learners for employment, entrepreneurship, and further technical or vocational education through hands-on training and performance-based activities.

Purpose of This Website

This website serves as an official platform of Alimodian National Comprehensive High School for the following purposes:

  • To showcase selected outputs and projects of TVL students.
  • To provide information about the TVL strand and its specializations.
  • To serve as a reference for documentation, assessment, and research.
  • To promote transparency and appreciation of students’ skills and achievements.

TVL Strand Specializations

The TVL strand of ANCHS offers the following specializations, aligned with industry-based skills and practical training:

  • Information and Communications Technology (ICT) – Web development, programming, and multimedia applications.
  • Computer Systems Servicing (CSS) – Computer assembly, repair, troubleshooting, and basic networking.
  • Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) – Welding techniques and metal fabrication.
  • Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM) – Electrical wiring, installation, and maintenance works.
  • Bread and Pastry Production (BPP) – Baking, pastry preparation, and food presentation.
  • Carpentry – Woodworking, furniture making, and basic construction.
  • Agricultural Crops Production – Crop cultivation and sustainable farming practices.
  • Animal Production – Livestock care and animal management.
  • Housekeeping – Hospitality services, cleanliness, and housekeeping operations.

Benefits of the TVL Strand

Learners enrolled in the TVL strand gain the following benefits:

  • Acquisition of practical and job-ready skills.
  • Preparation for employment and livelihood opportunities.
  • Exposure to industry-standard tools, equipment, and procedures.
  • Development of technical, problem-solving, and teamwork skills.
  • Creation of documented outputs for portfolios and academic requirements.
